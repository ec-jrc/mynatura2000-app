About the application (v-.-)

The purpose of this app is to provide information about the protected areas of the Natura 2000 network and enable you to send pictures collected in each site as well as providing an overall impression of your visit to each site. To select and view the protected areas you do not need to register and log in, but to send pictures and comments you need to go to the log in page first.

The pictures and comments provided by users are published here.
